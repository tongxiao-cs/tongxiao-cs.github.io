Hi all, my name is Tong XIAO. I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. I obtained my PhD degree from the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering, Hunan University, Changsha, China. My current research interests include automated log analysis, anomaly detection, failure prediction, and AIOps.
